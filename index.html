About The App

TREND Music App

TREND Music is a music player app similar to iTunes. To use the app, you must login first with your account credentials. If you do not have an account, you can sign up using the Register button, adding your account to the database. You have the My Library tab containing all your music and the Shop tab containing music you can buy and add to your own library. The music in the Sop tab cannot be played, you must buy and add them to your library first so that they can be played.
